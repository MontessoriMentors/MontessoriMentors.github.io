What We Do

Guiding the transformation of the adult in relation to the child. Providing a Montessori compass for parents, teachers and schools of children ages birth through six years.

We work both individually and as a team to model problem-solving, to ask questions and to reflect on specific solutions that will benefit the child, the adults, and the environment.

We work to foster sustainable changes in:

  1. In-home consultations, including devel-
    opment of home environments, promoting an understanding of developmental needs, and brainstorming and realizing strategies for intentional parenting
  2. Assisting the creation of new classrooms
    and programs, like Nidos, Young Children’s Communities, and Primary Classes
  3. Mentoring teachers and teacher teams

Our Mission

We believe it is important as Montessorians to work as a team, to model problem-solving, to ask questions and to reflect on specific solutions that will benefit the children, the adults and the environment, including the greater school community.

We believe in the crucial element of cultivating the culture of the school in the minds, attitudes and practices of the guides, and other staff members. When adults are united in this clear vision, we are building the foundation for Education for Peace.

We have over 70 years of combined work experience in our own classrooms and in our greater community.
